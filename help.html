Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first survey?

Creating your first survey is easy! Simply log in to your account, click the "Create Survey" button, and follow our step-by-step guide to design your perfect survey.

How can I share my survey?

You can share your survey through a direct link, email invitation, or embed it on your website. We also provide integration with various social media platforms.

What types of questions can I add?

We support multiple question types including multiple choice, rating scales, open-ended questions, matrix questions, and more. Each type can be customized to fit your needs.
